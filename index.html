Welcome

Located in the heart of Wilmington, North Carolina, this scenic city park offers a peaceful escape with beautiful landscapes and plenty of activities. Walking trails wind through tall oak trees and colorful flower gardens, leading to open fields. Relax by the pond or take in the breathtaking ocean views- Southridge Park has been the perfect place for outdoor fun since 1970.

Something for Everyone

Children can enjoy the playground and skate park, while joggers and cyclists make use of the paved paths. Friends and family can enjoy a meal at one of our many picnic tables. With something for all ages, our park a great place to relax, exercise, or spend time with loved ones.

Planning a special event? Southridge Park offers spacious shelters available for rent. Whether it's a birthday or a reunion, this park is a great spot for large parties and gatherings.

Location, Hours, and More

Whether you're planning a casual visit or looking to rent a shelter for a special event, the park staff is happy to help. For more information about amenities, reservations, or upcoming events, contact our park office.
