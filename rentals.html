Rent with Us!

Our park offers two shelters based on your needs! The South Shelter holds up to 50 people, making it great for small parties or family gatherings. The North Shelter is larger and can accommodate up to 150 people, perfect for big celebrations or events. Both shelters have picnic tables and grills for cooking. See the Shelter Rental Fee table below for pricing.

Rental Policies

Rentals are for a set time, and guests must clean up after their event. Loud music, alcohol, and open flames (except for park grills) are not allowed. To book a shelter, visitors must fill out the application form. After reviewing the application, the park will contact them to confirm details and discuss payment.
